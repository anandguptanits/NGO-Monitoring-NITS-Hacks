Quality Report

Each of the registered NGOs need to file their annual quality report which must include the below mentioned information.
1. Objectives set by the NGO at the beginning of the fiscal year and how much of it has been achieved.
2. Audit report of the last fiscal year.
3. Growth in terms of registered members, trained staff and capital investments.
4. An affidavit declaring that all the information provided above are true.
The uploaded report shall be an important factor in determining the feasibility of the funds/grants and permissions. This document is intended to bring about transparency in governance of NGOs. It will also send a pdf receipt as an acknowledgement to your registered email id.
